Fonts
Montserrat (Primary Display Font):


Light: Large headers.
Medium: Smaller headers.
Bold: Subheaders.
Usage: For headers, subheaders, and callouts. Not for body text.
Roboto Condensed:


Light: Subheaders.
Regular: Body text.
Bold: Captions.
Usage: Body text, captions, and subheaders (if needed). Not for main headers.
Roboto Serif:


Regular: Alternative for body text in formal settings.
Arial:


Usage: General text in emails or letters.
Font Guidelines:
Larger point size: Thinner weight.
Smaller point size: Bolder weight.
Colors
Core Colors:


PPPL Orange: #f58025 (C0 M61 Y97 K0)
Accent color for rules, underlines, symbols, and sparing use in text or text backgrounds.
PPPL Grey: #405965 (C30 M0 Y0 K75)
Primary base color for header backgrounds and bold header text. Tints can soften contrast.
PPPL Magenta: #c12d63 (C24 M97 Y46 K0)
Accent color for highlighting or distinguishing elements.
PPPL Yellow: #ffdd00 (C0 M10 Y100 K0)
Accent color for highlighting or distinguishing elements.
PPPL Cyan: #008c9d (C80 M0 Y20 K30)
Accent color for highlighting or distinguishing elements.
Gradients:


Grey transitions to lighter variants or cyan for headers or larger areas.
Grey-to-orange and orange-to-yellow gradients match the logo.
Orange gradients can include magenta for intensity and are ideal for thicker rules.
Color Usage:
White or Grey: Base color for headers and backgrounds.
Orange: Accent color, sparingly for text or text backgrounds.
Magenta, Yellow, Cyan: Accent colors for highlighting or distinguishing elements.
